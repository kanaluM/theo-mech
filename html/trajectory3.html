Free-fall projectile in 1D

Consider the case of a projectile in free fall in 1D. Suppose our boundary conditions are that the projectile travels to and from (0, 0) in 20 seconds (parameterized from \(t=0\) to \(t=20\)). Given a parameterized path of the form \[y(t) = y_{sol}(t) + \Delta y(t)\] \[y(t) = t(20-t) + \alpha (t-t_1)(t-t_2)(t-t_3)\] \[y(t) = 20t-t^2 + \alpha (t^3-t_1t^2-t_2t^2-t_3t^2+t_1t_2t+t_2t_3t+t_1t_3t-t_1t_2t_3)\] \[y(t) = \alpha t^3 + (-1-\alpha(t_1+t_2+t_3))t^2 + (\alpha(t_1t_2+t_2t_3+t_1t_3)+20)t-\alpha t_1t_2t_3\] \[y(t) = at^3 + bt^2 +ct +d\]
