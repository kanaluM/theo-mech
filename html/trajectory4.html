Free-fall projectile in 1D

Consider the case of a projectile in free fall in 1D. Suppose our boundary conditions are that the projectile travels to and from (0, 0) in 20 seconds (parameterized from \(t=0\) to \(t=20\)). Furthermore, suppose that we fixed the point \(t=10\) as \((0,10)\).
